This tileset was inspired by Spirited Away and other Studio Ghibli films. Growing up, these were some of my favorites. As I got older, I grew more impressed by their visual artistry. I attempted to capture the essence of these films in this tileset through the careful selection of colors and an attempt to match the delicate line-work. I also included icons which represent key elements of the films which are placed at locations that are either inspired by or inspiration for Studio Ghibli films.
